February’s Bible Story: NOAH’S ARK

2017-02-10
past packs & boxes
Share

This month HelloBible focused on the fact that God protected Noah’s family and the animals. We also accented the rainbow, the sign of God’s promise to Noah.

Our journey lead us to the Story of Noah’s Ark. We learned more about God’s plan with Noah and the animals. Our Bible explorer’s read the storybook “A man named Noah”. Each explorer built an awesome ark and a matching game which presents the fact that the animals came into the ark two by two. They also made beautiful giant rainbow bubbles to remind them of the promise God gave to Noah that he never sends such a flood again. The families also went to a park to measure how huge the ark really was, according to the dimensions given in the Bible.
