Together we can make an impact

Each HelloBible box helps provide education for children in Kilgoris, Kenya.

The Kilgoris Project operates schools, provides daily food and clean water. Many students are the first in their families to attend school.

Your purchase helps! Here’s some numbers:

  • 25 HelloBible boxes send two children to primary school for three month.
  • 100 HelloBible boxes pay a teacher’s salary for one month in Kilgoris.
Teach your child the importance of giving. Make an impact.
