The Story Behind HelloBible

HelloBible creates monthly experiences for Christian families to grow in faith and thrive in relationship with Jesus.

"Let your roots grow down into Him, and let your lives be built on Him. Then your faith will grow strong in the truth you were taught, and you will overflow with thankfulness."

Colossians 2:7

Founder. Connie.

I am a Christian mom of three and a teacher with specialization in Biblical studies. Faith is an important part of my life. I am involved in a church community and lead a Bible study for moms. As my children grew older, I longed to nurture their faith and give them a solid spiritual foundation.

My struggle. How can I help my children to grow in faith?

I found myself searching for ways to express faith more vibrantly in our home. But I struggled in the midst of our busy family life. It took effort to plan creative and engaging activities and it was challenging to find time to buy all the supplies. When discussing this with my friends, I realized that I was not alone in this struggle. Many of us long to help our children grow in faith and thrive in relationship with Jesus.

Solution. HelloBible.

The idea of a monthly subscription service for families was born out of a desire to help Christian families. God opened many doors to start HelloBible.

HelloBible provides your family with materials to help you teach, learn, and grow in faith. Each month, HelloBible focuses on a specific Bible story. Each box contains a storybook, parent guides, and interactive craft projects to explore the Bible with your children at home.
