  • The Idea

    Once we met on a playground, we soon became friends. We are moms, christians, and both have a professional background in education. Growing up in a christian family and learning about christian holidays and values has always been a very important part of our lifes. As parents, we also want to introduce our children to the beauty of the Bible and to talk about faith in our day to day life. Unfortunately, our busy family life makes this goal hard to achieve. Wouldn’t it be awesome to get Bible crafts and ideas pre-made on a regular basis? Yes, it totally would!

  • Launching HelloBible

    Connie’s passion for teaching and designing hands-on materials and Simone’s experience as a social worker assembles a perfect team. Also, being moms helped us to make this idea a reality. Launching HelloBible is a big answer to our prayers. GOD is amazing! HelloBible changed our life and we love what we do.

  • Helping Children in Need

    Another important part of HelloBible is to help children in need by donating a portion of each box. We partner with a great organization called THE KILGORIS PROJECT which educates and feeds children in Kenya. We are very grateful that God gives us the opportunity to be a blessing to these children and to get to know more about their life and their needs.

  • Our Mission

    We pray that your HelloBible box brings fun, joy and valuable time into your home each month. A good friend said, “Getting a HelloBible box is like receiving a gift, that enables you to explore God’s great love in many ways.” Our mission is to send this precious gift to you. We are looking forward to welcoming you into our awesome HelloBible community.
