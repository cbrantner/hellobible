The Noah Box

Welcome card with Monthly Bible Verse.

Each box comes with a memory verse card. It helps children keep God's Word in their heart.

Read the Story Book.

The fun begins with a faith-filled Bible Story. The Noah Box comes with the book "A Man Named Noah" from Arch books. Read and enjoy the story as a family.

Child friendly Instructions.

All crafts and activities come with clear and attractive directions for assembly. Older children can easily follow by themselves. Younger children can follow with very little assistance.

Build Noah's Ark.

The box contains all materials to color and create an ark out of cardstock and a wooden base.

Family Activity. Noah Matching Game.

Noah needed two of every animal to fill his ark. The box contains wooden chips and animal stickers to create a fun matching game. Playing the game is a great family activity. Each time you find a matching pair, place them into the ark.

Make Giant Rainbow Bubbles.

"I will put my rainbow in the clouds to be a sign of my promise to the earth." Genesis 9:13. Make giant rainbow bubbles to remember God's promise.

Easy-to-teach Parent Guides.

Parent guides have discussion questions to help children understand the Bible story. They include prayer, fun facts, art projects, and additional ideas to help kids learn and engage with the lesson.
