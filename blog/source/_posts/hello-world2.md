We are happy to announce an exciting partnership with [The Kilgoris Project](http://kilgoris.org/). For every HelloBible box purchased, we will donate 10% to this wonderful project.  The Kilgoris Project educates and cares for school-aged children in rural Kenya, partnering with the community to build rising futures. More than 180 students expand their horizons and learn teamwork and responsibility through a variety of enrichment programs. The integrated health and wellness programs play a critical role. Every child has the basic health care they need to thrive in school.
